## 2. Brand Identity & Logo

### 2.1 Logo Mark

The Ocha Wear logo is a **serif monogram "OC"** with overlapping letterforms on a solid olive green background. Key characteristics:

- **Form:** Two overlapping serif capitals — "O" and "C" — sharing negative space at their intersection
- **Style:** Classical, elegant, high-contrast serif — reminiscent of luxury fashion monograms
- **Feeling:** Sophisticated, timeless, understated confidence

### 2.2 Logo Colors (Extracted)

| Element | Color Name | Hex | RGB |
|---|---|---|---|
| Background | Ocha Olive | `#768156` | rgb(118, 129, 86) |
| Lettermark | Ocha Cream | `#EAE4DA` | rgb(234, 228, 218) |

### 2.3 Logo Usage Rules

- **Minimum size:** 32px (digital), 10mm (print)
- **Clear space:** Equal to the cap-height of the lettermark on all sides
- **Approved versions:**
  - Olive background + Cream lettermark (primary)
  - Cream background + Olive lettermark (reversed)
  - Solid Olive on transparent (single-color use)
- **Never** place the logo on busy photographic backgrounds without a solid color block behind it
- **Never** recolor the logo outside the two approved color combinations
- **Never** stretch, skew, or add effects (shadow, outline, glow) to the logo

## 5. Visual Identity

### 5.1 Aesthetic Direction

Ocha Wear's visual identity is grounded by an **elegant, nature-rooted sophistication** — the olive and cream logo signals that this is a brand with taste and restraint, not just trend-chasing. The aesthetic bridges:

- **Everyday accessibility** (affordable, relatable, community-driven)
- **Quiet elegance** (monogram logo, serif sensibility, warm neutrals)

Key visual descriptors:
- Warm, earthy naturalism — olive, cream, warm neutrals as the foundation
- Seasonal campaign palettes layered on top of a stable, sophisticated base
- Light and airy photography with warm natural tones
- Clean, model-forward compositions
- Restrained use of decorative elements — elegance through simplicity


### 5.4 Typography

| Usage | Typeface | Weight | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Display / Hero | Cormorant Garamond | 400 Italic, 600 | Headlines, collection names |
| Body | DM Sans | 400, 500 | All body copy, UI labels |
| Script Accent | Satisfy | 400 | Decorative only — taglines, stamps |

The serif display choice aligns directly with the classical serif of the logo monogram. The logo's letterform aesthetic should be echoed in editorial headings.

**Recommended pairings:**
- **Headings:** Cormorant Garamond Italic (mirrors logo's serif character)
- **Body:** DM Sans (clean, modern contrast to the display face)
- **Accent:** Satisfy — used sparingly for collection launch labels only

### 5.5 Photography Style

- **Model photography:** Warm natural light; neutral, earthy backgrounds preferred
- **Color treatment:** Warm-lifted, slightly desaturated greens — consistent with olive palette
- **Flat lays:** Cream or warm off-white surface, minimal props
- **Mood:** Elevated yet accessible — aspirational but not unattainable
- **Never:** Cool-toned, moody, or high-contrast dark photography
